## Problem

Brian's Freedom Roadmap requires weekly tracking of: leads generated, offers made, deals under contract, deals closed, cash on hand, nest egg balance, debt remaining. Currently the analytics page shows property/signal stats but not these business KPIs.

## Solution

- Add a "Weekly Scorecard" section to analytics or a new accountability page
- Track per week: leads generated, offers made, contracts signed, deals closed
- Financial tracking: cash on hand, nest egg balance (from Babylon allocations), debt remaining
- Show week-over-week trends
- Daily goals reference: 50 calls, 10 conversations, 3 leads, 2 offers (from sales training)
