## Mid-Plan Pivot Note

The phase executed through a significant pivot during Plan 02. The original Plan 01 design used a city-name allowlist (`SLC_84116_CITIES`) to filter SLC notices to 84116. Two dry-run iterations revealed this was impossible:

1. First dry-run: all 145 SLC notices show `city="Salt Lake City"` regardless of property location — the city field reflects the law firm's jurisdiction, not the property neighborhood.
2. Second iteration (snippet-zip extraction): 0 of 145 SLC notices contained a property zip code in the 300-char truncated snippet.

Brian approved **Option C-2**: build a UGRC Address Points → 84116 parcel-ID allowlist, filter SLC notices by parcel-ID match. This is the live implementation. All success criteria are evaluated against the final Option C-2 implementation.
